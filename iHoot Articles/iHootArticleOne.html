What are the 7 sisters?



The 7 sisters are a group of colleges made in the late 19th century just for girls (it was like the girl Ivies). You might be thinking Vassar was made by a girl because it was an all-girls school, but it was not!!

What?



Vassar was made by Matthew Vassar in 1861. A man. His niece Lydia Booth encouraged him to make a college for girls in Poughkeepsie New York. He built the school with $408,000 dollars (that would be 9.7 million dollars today's money).
